Frequently Asked

Is the restaurant on the moon open to the public or limited to astronauts?

Our restaurant on the moon is open to the public! Anyone can enjoy the unique dining experience it offers.

How does food preparation and cooking work in a low-gravity environment?

Our chefs have perfected techniques to adapt to the low-gravity environment. Specialized equipment, food containers, and cooking methods are utilized to ensure food is cooked and served safely and deliciously.

Are there any restrictions on bringing personal items or souvenirs from the moon restaurant back to Earth?

Due to international space regulations, we regret to inform you that bringing personal items or souvenirs from the moon restaurant back to Earth is not currently permitted.

Can I have a view of the Earth while dining at the restaurant?

Absolutely! Our lunar restaurant features expansive windows that provide breathtaking views of the Earth, allowing you to savor your meal while gazing at our beautiful planet.

How much would it cost for an average person to travel to the Moon restaurant?

The cost of traveling to the Moon is undoubtedly a significant investment due to the complexity and resources required for lunar voyages. As of now, considering the nature of our restaurant and the unique experience it offers, the estimated cost for an average person to embark on a lunar journey and dine at our extraordinary establishment is set at $999,999,990. This price encompasses the comprehensive travel package, including the spacecraft transportation, spacesuit rental, accommodations, lunar activities, and the ultimate dining experience at our lunar restaurant.

How long does it take to travel from Earth to the moon restaurant?

Travel times can vary depending on several factors, such as the trajectory taken, and any potential layovers or docking procedures en route. The duration of the trip is continuously being improved as space exploration technology advances. Currently, the average travel time from Earth to our lunar restaurant is approximately 3 days using specialized spacecraft.

Are there any special events or entertainment options available at the moon restaurant?

Yes! We host special lunar events and offer unique entertainment experiences such as stargazing sessions, zero-gravity performances, and moonwalk tours led by experienced guides.

How is dining even possible while wearing a spacesuit?

At our lunar restaurant, we have specially designed spacesuit-friendly dining options. The spacesuits we provide are equipped with airtight and flexible feeding ports near the helmet area. These ports allow you to enjoy your meal by inserting easy-to-consume food items through the designated openings. Our culinary team has created a delightful menu that ensures a seamless dining experience even within the constraints of spacesuits.
